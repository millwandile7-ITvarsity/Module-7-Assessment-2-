about us

The Wandi's Food Joint Restaurant, located on Park Town, specializes in delicious, homemade foods and warm, friendly service. We’re all family here at the Wandi's Food Joint, with sisters, parents, and husbands working together to provide our customers with nothing but the best: quality foods made with quality ingredients, served in a comfortable, inviting place. That’s why locals and visitors alike have been joining us for nearly twenty years, both here and at our sister locations, the Hole in One Donut Shop in Eastham, and the Hole Donut Shop and Deli in Orleans. We are committed to our customers and the Jozi community we love, and show our support for local schools and community fundraisers whenever possible. Come join us at our upcoming events! Please stop by and see us next time you’re on the Jozi. We promise it won’t be your last!
